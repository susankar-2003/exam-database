1. You meet two inhabitants of an island, Alice and Bob. Alice says, "We are both knaves." Knaves always lie and knights always tell the truth. What are Alice and Bob?
A) Alice is a knight, Bob is a knave
B) Alice is a knave, Bob is a knight
C) Both are knights
D) Both are knaves
Ans: B
2. You meet two inhabitants, Charles and Diana. Charles says, "At least one of us is a knave." What are Charles and Diana?
A) Charles is a knight, Diana is a knave
B) Charles is a knave, Diana is a knight
C) Both are knights
D) Both are knaves
Ans: A
3. You meet two inhabitants, Edward and Fiona. Edward says, "I am a knave or Fiona is a knight." What are Edward and Fiona?

C) Both are knights
D) Both are knaves
Ans: C
