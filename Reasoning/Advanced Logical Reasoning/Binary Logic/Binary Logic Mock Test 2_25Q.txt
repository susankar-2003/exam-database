1. A unicorn lies on Thu/Fri/Sat. He says: "I told the truth yesterday and I will tell the truth tomorrow." What day is it?
A) Thursday
B) Friday
C) Saturday
D) Sunday
Ans: B
2. You meet three inhabitants, M, N, and O. M says, "N is a knave." N says, "O is a knave." O says, "Both M and N are knaves." How many are knaves?
A) 1
B) 2
C) 3
D) 0
Ans: B
3. Three suspects A, B, C are interrogated. Exactly one is guilty and always lies. The innocent ones always tell the truth. A says "B is guilty." B says "C is innocent." C says "A is innocent." Who is guilty?
A) A
B) B
C) C
D) None of them
Ans: A
4. Three suspects A, B, C. Exactly two are guilty and they always lie. The innocent always tells the truth. A says "B is innocent." B says "C is guilty." C says "A is guilty." Who is innocent?
A) A
B) B
C) C
D) Cannot be determined
Ans: B
5. You are in a maze with two talking statues. One always lies, one always tells the truth. You ask Statue 1: "If I ask you if the exit is left, will you say yes?" Statue 1 says "Yes." Which way is the exit?
A) Left
B) Right
C) Straight
D) Cannot be determined
