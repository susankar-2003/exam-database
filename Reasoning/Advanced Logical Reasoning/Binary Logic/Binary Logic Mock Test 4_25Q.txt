1. A says, "I am a knave and B is a knight." What is B?
A) Knight
B) Knave
C) Normal
D) Cannot be determined
Ans: B
2. A says, "I am a knave or B is a knave." What are they?
A) A is a knight, B is a knight
B) A is a knight, B is a knave
C) A is a knave, B is a knight
D) A is a knave, B is a knave
Ans: B
3. A says, "If I am a knight, then B is a knave." B says, "A is a knave." What are they?
A) A is a knight, B is a knight
B) A is a knight, B is a knave
C) A is a knave, B is a knight
D) A is a knave, B is a knave
Ans: B
4. A says, "B is a knight." B says, "If A is a knight, then I am a knave." What are they?
A) A is a knight, B is a knight
B) A is a knight, B is a knave
C) A is a knave, B is a knight
D) A is a knave, B is a knave
Ans: D
5. A says, "Either I am a knave or B is a knight." B says nothing. What are they?
A) A is a knight, B is a knight
B) A is a knight, B is a knave
C) A is a knave, B is a knight
D) A is a knave, B is a knave
Ans: A

A) A
B) B
C) C
D) None
