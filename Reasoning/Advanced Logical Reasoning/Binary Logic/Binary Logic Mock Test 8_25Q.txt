1. M says, "N and O are both truth-tellers." N says, "M is a liar and O is a truth-teller." O says, "M and N are both liars." Who is a truth-teller?
A) M
B) N
C) O
D) None
Ans: C
2. P says, "Q is a liar." Q says, "R is a liar." R says, "S is a liar." S says, "P is a liar." Which pair could both be liars?
A) P and Q
B) P and R
C) P and S
D) Q and S
Ans: B
3. T says, "I am a truth-teller." U says, "T is a liar." V says, "U is a liar." W says, "V is a liar." If exactly two are truth-tellers, who are they?
A) T and U
B) T and V
C) U and W
D) V and W
Ans: B
4. X says, "Y is a truth-teller." Y says, "Z is a liar." Z says, "W is a liar." W says, "X, Y, and Z are all liars." Who is a truth-teller?
A) X
B) Y
C) Z
D) W
Ans: C
5. A1 says, "B1 is a liar." B1 says, "C1 is a liar." C1 says, "A1 and B1 are of the same type." What is C1?
A) Truth-teller
B) Liar
C) Normal
D) Cannot be determined
Ans: B
6. D1 says, "If E1 is a truth-teller, then I am a liar." What must be true?
A) D1 is a truth-teller
B) E1 is a truth-teller
C) E1 is a liar
D) Both are liars
Ans: C
7. F1 says, "G1 and H1 are of the same type." G1 says, "F1 and H1 are of different types." What is H1?
A) Truth-teller
B) Liar
C) Alternator
D) Cannot be determined
Ans: B
8. I1 says, "Exactly one of us (I1, J1, K1) is a truth-teller." J1 says, "Exactly one of us is a liar." K1 says, "All of us are liars." Who is a truth-teller?
A) I1
B) J1
C) K1
D) Both I1 and J1
Ans: A
9. L1 says, "M1 is a liar." M1 says, "N1 is a liar." N1 says, "O1 is a liar." O1 says, "L1 is a liar." Which statement is correct?
A) Exactly one is a truth-teller
B) Exactly two are truth-tellers

A) X1
B) Y1
C) Z1
D) None

14. A machine has three indicator lights that can be True or False. Light A: "Light B is False." Light B: "Light C is False." Light C: "Light A is False." How many lights are True?
A) 0
B) 1
C) 2
D) 3

D) 3
