1. If all roses are flowers and some flowers are red, which conclusion definitely follows?
   A) All roses are red
   B) Some roses are red
   C) All red things are roses
   D) All roses are flowers
   Ans: D

2. If Ravi is taller than Amit and Amit is taller than Sunil, who is the shortest?
   A) Ravi
   B) Amit
   C) Sunil
   D) Cannot be determined
   Ans: C
