1. If you eat too much candy, you get a stomach ache. You have a stomach ache. Therefore, you ate too much candy. This is:
A) Valid Modus Ponens
B) Valid Modus Tollens
C) Affirming the Consequent
D) Denying the Antecedent
Ans: C

2. If a fruit is an apple, it grows on a tree. This fruit is not an apple. Therefore, it does not grow on a tree. This is:
A) Valid
B) Denying the Antecedent
C) Modus Tollens
D) Affirming the Consequent
Ans: B
