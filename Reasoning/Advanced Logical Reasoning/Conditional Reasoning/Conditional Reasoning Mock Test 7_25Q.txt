1. If you play with fire, you get burned. You are not burned. Conclusion: You did not play with fire. This is:
A) Modus Ponens
B) Modus Tollens
C) Affirming the Consequent
D) Denying the Antecedent
Ans: B

2. If you are a mammal, you have a heart. A whale is a mammal. Conclusion: A whale has a heart. This is:
A) Modus Ponens
B) Modus Tollens
C) Affirming the Consequent

Ans: A
