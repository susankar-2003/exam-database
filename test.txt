Q: Which logic gate outputs 1 only when all inputs are 1?
A: OR
B: AND
C: NOT
D: NAND
ANS: B

Q: What is the standard unit of electrical resistance?
A: Ampere
B: Volt
C: Ohm
D: Watt
ANS: C

Q: In a p-n junction diode, the depletion region is created by:
A: Ionization
B: Diffusion of charge carriers
C: Applied voltage
D: Creation of electron-hole pairs
ANS: B
